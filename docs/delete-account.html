Delete your Camp Trader account

You can delete your account and request deletion of associated personal data.

How to delete in the app

  1. Open the Camp Trader app → Menu → Account → Delete account.
  2. Confirm. You’ll be signed out and your account will be scheduled for removal.

If you can’t access the app

Email support@camptrader.app from your account email with subject “Delete my account”. We’ll verify and process your request.

What we delete

  • Account profile (email, display name), authentication identifier
  • Your listings and messages (unless law requires retention)

What may be retained

  • Legal, fraud-prevention, and audit records as permitted by law
  • Aggregated, de-identified metrics that cannot identify you

Timing

Processing begins within 7 days of verification; legal/audit retention may extend specific items.

Questions? Contact support@camptrader.app.
