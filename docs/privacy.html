Camp Trader – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-29 • Contact: support@camptrader.app
Camp Trader is an Android mobile app. It is not available on the web.

1) Scope

This policy explains how the Camp Trader Android app (“App”) collects, uses, and shares information. The App requires only a display name, an email address, and a general location for classified ads. Other personal details shown in the profile section (such as phone number, date of birth, gender, or camping preferences) are optional and provided only if you choose to share them.

2) We Don’t Sell Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

3) What We Collect (and why)

  • Display name and email address – to create and manage your account, authenticate login, and provide support. (Required)
  • General location (approximate area or truncated coordinates) – required when posting classified ads so listings can be shown by region or nearby area.
  • Optional profile data (phone number, date of birth, gender, camp style, or similar) – voluntarily provided to personalize your profile; not needed to use the App.
  • User-generated content (listings, photos, messages) – used to operate the marketplace and messaging features.
  • Device identifiers (installation ID, messaging token) – used for notifications, integrity, and fraud prevention.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics – collected automatically to improve stability and performance.

4) Optional vs. Required

Only your display name, email, and general location are required to post listings and use the App. All other profile fields are optional and can be left blank without affecting your ability to use the App.

5) How We Use Data

  • Operate and secure the App (accounts, listings, messaging, notifications)
  • Prevent fraud and abuse; debug and fix crashes
  • Communicate about service updates or support issues

We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

6) Location Display for Listings

Listing maps display approximate coordinates (about one-kilometer radius) to reduce precision. You may not omit general location when posting a classified ad.

7) Sharing & Service Providers

We share data only with trusted service providers that help us operate the App—such as cloud hosting, authentication, messaging, analytics, and crash-reporting platforms. They process data solely on our behalf and under confidentiality obligations. We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect rights or safety.

8) Security

We use industry-standard safeguards and encrypt data in transit. Avoid including sensitive information in listings or chats.

9) Retention & Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account or data by contacting support@camptrader.app or using the in-app delete option.

10) Cookies / Web

The Camp Trader app does not use cookies, web trackers, or external analytics tools. It is not available on the web.

11) Children

Camp Trader is not directed to children. If our audience changes, we will update this policy.

12) Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, or correct your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

13) Changes

We may update this policy. Updates will be posted with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use after changes means you accept the new policy.
